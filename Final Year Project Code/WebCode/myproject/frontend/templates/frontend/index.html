About Us

Buckle up for an unforgettable adventure as we embark on a once-in-a-lifetime trip to Daikoku PA, home to Japan's vibrant car culture and breathtaking sights. Picture yourself cruising in my iconic Nissan R34 Skyline, the perfect blend of speed and style, as we glide along the scenic coastal roads. As we approach Daikoku PA, the anticipation builds, and you'll be captivated by the stunning views of Tokyo Bay.

The shimmering skyline serves as a picturesque backdrop, and the scent of fresh ocean air fills our lungs, setting the stage for an incredible day ahead. Along the way, we'll stop at hidden gems like the breathtaking Shonan coastline, where you can capture the beauty of the azure waves crashing against the cliffs and snap photos that will leave your friends in awe.

At Daikoku PA, get ready to immerse yourself in the electrifying atmosphere of this automotive paradise. As we park the R34 among a stunning collection of unique vehicles, you'll witness car enthusiasts showcasing their prized rides, each telling a story of passion and dedication.
